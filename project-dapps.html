What Are the Advantages of Dapps?
Many clients highlight four positive aspects concerning decentralized applications. First, they are apart from censorship because nobody, including influential people and governments, can manage them. Second, they go on functioning even when a person’s device fails, thanks to the utilization of the peer-to-peer network. Third, they are based on blockchain, so it is not a problem to use cryptocurrency for Dapps. Finally, they are open-source, so promoters easily add new functions and develop the Dapp ecosystem.

What Are the Negative Sides of Dapps?.
Dapps are very promising, but they also have disadvantages.
1. Terms and conditions are executed automatically, thanks to smart contracts. It makes Dapps vulnerable to hacking because the fraudsters do their best to find network weaknesses.
2. Some Dapps have poorly developed interfaces, so clients often stop using them. Nevertheless, developers try to change this negative aspect and enhance the customer’s usability.
3. Dapps cannot be so interactive because of a few customers who have no power to improve the delivered services. As a rule, when apps have many users, they are regarded as safe. Dapps, on the opposite, can hardly prove their security.

Dapps

Play Market and Apple Store are full of applications that people use daily. Dapps are also applications, but they are built on blockchains using smart contracts. This approach lets creators develop products that will be sold or bought without third parties. Due to that, a buyer does not have to pay a fee to the middleman for his services because all products are decentralized.

A responsible person controls the price and availability of apps on markets. Neither a single person nor an organization can control Dapps because they are developed on blockchains that guarantee anonymity. For Dapps we’re usually provide the following features. Since all records and data are open-source, nobody can control them, and they run independently. Their information is open to the public.
