NFT gamification
NFT gamification includes typical game elements such as scoring, competitions, and game rules but uses blockchain technology to implement them. This combination has led to the emergence of GameFi, where users trade and exchange game assets for financial rewards. In fact, the principle of NFT games is quite simple. Assets used in the game world are NFTs that have value for players and collectors and can be traded. For example, in the game Axie Infinity, each Axie creature is an NFT token. Players can fight with their NFTs and earn cryptocurrency rewards.


How the Metaverse Differs from Regular Online Games
Settlements within the metaverse take place in cryptocurrency. They are traded on exchanges like Coinbase: you can buy crypto at any time for dollars and start spending it inside the metaverse, for example, buying and selling virtual land, houses, clothes, and other objects. You can also do the opposite: earn cryptocurrency within the metaverse to later sell it for euros on the stock exchange, withdraw it to your bank card and spend it in the supermarket.


Hashing
Hashing is a cryptographic method of converting large amounts of data into short values that are difficult to fake. It is a crucial component of blockchain technology regarding the security and integrity of the data flowing through the system.

Decentralized financial services (Decentralized Finance, Defi) have begun to embrace the field of blockchain games. Now you can not only play on these platforms but also collect rewards from NFTs to receive more income in the future. Let's discuss the most promising Defi gaming platforms that use these technologies. Players can start by collecting or buying game assets for their favorite blockchain games.

To help players find NFT items and character opportunities and secure the future of the gaming metaverse, the NFT has launched a new gaming initiative, the Initial Game Token Offering, or IGO. IGO Collections will launch weekly on the NFT marketplace. These will include exclusive in-game NFTs: early access to in-game assets such as weapons, items, exclusive Binance cosmetics, skins, and more.
